Sudoku

    Rules:

  • The numbers 1, 2, 3 and 4 must occur in each column and row but only ONCE per row and column.
  • The numbers 1-4 can only occur once in each of the four boxes marked with the same color.
  • Once you've completely filled in your answers, press the Enter button!
  • The answers that are correct will change to text, the incorrect boxes will stay the same.
  • You only have once chance to press Enter! Be sure of your answers before you do so.
